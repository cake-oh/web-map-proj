International Fishing in the Pacific Ocean

Global fishing activities are expanding across the world's oceans.
Coastal countries have sovereign rights over the ocean area 200 nautical miles out from their coastlines; these national waters are called Exclusive Economic Zones (EEZs).
Much fishing takes place inside EEZs but also outside them in the world's areas beyond national jurisdiction, also called the high seas.
