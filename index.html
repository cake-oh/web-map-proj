International Fishing in the Pacific Ocean

As global fishing activities grow, countries are increasingly fishing in the same spaces.
This could potentially lead to spatial competition over fishing grounds,
in both national waters (Exclusive Economic Zones) and areas beyond national jurisdiction (high seas).
Because of this, it may be important to visualize where these crowded areas are occurring.

In the map below, you can toggle the two layers (bottom map corners) to visualize regions with
high international fishing activity, in terms of both international fishing diversity and fishing effort.
